✨ Astraea — Privacy Policy

A private AI assistant application on Discord

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Astraea is a private, invite-only Discord application operated by its owner (the “Operator”) for personal use. This policy explains what data the application handles and why. Astraea is not affiliated with Discord Inc. or Anthropic PBC.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Astraea on Discord, the application receives and may store:

  • Discord account information — your user ID, username, and display name;
  • Message data — the content of messages you send in direct messages with the application, or in server channels the application has been invited to, along with timestamps and channel/server identifiers;
  • Attachments — files or media you send to the application.

Astraea does not collect email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or precise location data, and does not use cookies or trackers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To generate responses and carry out tasks you request;
  • To maintain conversation history so the assistant has context across sessions;
  • To operate, debug, and secure the Service (e.g., logs used to diagnose failures and to restrict the Service to authorized users).

Your data is never sold, rented, or used for advertising, marketing, or training of machine-learning models by the Operator.

3. AI Processing

To generate responses, message content is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. That processing is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy and commercial terms. No other third party receives your data, except services you explicitly ask the assistant to use on your behalf (for example, pushing code you requested to a code-hosting service).

4. Storage and Security

Conversation logs and assistant memory are stored on hardware controlled by the Operator, not on shared cloud infrastructure. Access is limited to the Operator. Credentials and API keys are stored separately from conversation data. No system is perfectly secure, but reasonable measures are taken to protect the data described above.

5. Data Retention

Conversation history is retained for as long as it is useful for providing continuity in the Service, or until you request its deletion. Operational logs may be retained for a shorter period for debugging.

6. Your Rights

You may request, at any time:

  • a summary of the data the Service holds about you;
  • correction or deletion of that data;
  • that the Service stop processing your messages entirely.

Send any such request by direct message to the Astraea application on Discord; it will reach the Operator. Requests are honored within a reasonable time, typically days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required by local law) and does not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact the Operator and it will be deleted.

8. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be available at this URL, with the effective date shown above.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or requests can be sent to the Operator by direct message to the Astraea application on Discord.
